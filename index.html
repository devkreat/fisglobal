About Us

GreenLight Express Customs Brokerage is a leading provider of full-service customs brokerage for global ocean and air cargo. We believe that personalized service and more than a decade of experience is the two major contributors to any successful shipment clearance.
At GreenLight Express Customs Brokerage, we understand the need and urgency of your shipments and we will ensure there is no delay in clearing your shipment through U.S. Customs to you.
